# Lecturer's Office Door Design

## Visual Layout

The door now looks like a real university lecturer's office door with the following elements:

### 1. Room Number (Top)
- **Position**: Top center of the door
- **Style**: Brass/gold plaque with "ROOM" label
- **Content**: Shows the ranking order (e.g., "ROOM #1", "ROOM #2")
- **Design**: Amber gradient background with brown border

### 2. Match Score Badge (Below Room Number)
- **Position**: Just below the room number
- **Style**: Circular green badge
- **Content**: Compatibility percentage (e.g., "85% Match")
- **Design**: Green gradient with white text

### 3. Nameplate (Center of Door)
- **Position**: Center of the door
- **Style**: Traditional brass nameplate
- **Design**: Cream/amber background with brown border
- **Content**:
  - **Teacher Name** (bold, large text)
  - **Subject** (smaller, below name)
  - **Tagline** (italic quote)
  - **Summary** (small description text)
  - **Archetype** (badge at bottom)

### 4. Door Handle
- **Position**: Right side, middle
- **Style**: Gold/brass horizontal handle

## Color Scheme

- **Door**: Rich brown wood gradient (#8B4513 to #654321)
- **Border**: Dark brown (#5C3317)
- **Room Number**: Amber/gold (#FCD34D)
- **Match Badge**: Green gradient (#4ADE80 to #16A34A)
- **Nameplate**: Cream/amber (#FFFBEB to #FEF3C7)
- **Text**: Dark brown (#78350F, #92400E)

## Dimensions

- **Door Size**: 300px wide × 480px tall
- **Carousel Height**: 700px (to accommodate taller doors)
- **Nameplate**: 90% of door width

## Information Hierarchy

1. **Room Number** (Ranking) - Most prominent at top
2. **Match Score** - Eye-catching green badge
3. **Teacher Name** - Bold header in nameplate
4. **Subject** - Secondary info
5. **Tagline** - Personality quote
6. **Summary** - Detailed description
7. **Archetype** - Category badge

## Interactive States

- **Center Door**: Shows all information (room number, match score, nameplate)
- **Side Doors**: Blurred, no text visible (maintains mystery)
- **Hover**: Cursor changes to pointer on center door
- **Click**: Triggers door swing animation (90° rotation)

## Design Philosophy

The design mimics a real university corridor where:
- Each lecturer has their own office
- Room numbers indicate ranking/order
- Nameplates provide professional information
- Students can "visit" by clicking the door
- The aesthetic is warm, academic, and inviting
